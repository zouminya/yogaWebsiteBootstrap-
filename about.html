Who Am I?

My name is Min Zou, a yogi for over 5 years now. I had hunchback due to poor postures and suffered from lower back pain becaus of sitting all day at work. Yoga helps me so I love to advocate for yoga. However, I am not a licensed yoga teacher. Please follow your doctor's advises and suggestion. Yoga poses can be modified according to your strength, experience and condition. Keep in mind, never push yourself further into any poses than what feels comfortable. You can get hurt. Flexibility and strength will come but it takes time. If you are new to yoga and want to try yoga, try to take the in-person instructed classes.
